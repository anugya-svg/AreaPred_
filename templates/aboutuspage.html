About Us

The question that arises when walking in the streets, sitting in a quiet place to drink a cup of coffee or get on the bus to go home is, Is this place safe?
To answer this question we had prepared a platform which will definitely helps you and even your one time experience can help millions of people to stay safe and secure at the time to unknown place
Prediction of area (safe or unsafe) using Machine Learning.
This project is using Machine Learning to predict whether a given place in a given time is safe to go or not. The datasets which we have collected is fed into the machine learning model to predict the outcome.

The prediction is done based on various features(parameters) including but not limited to Place
Zone
Time
People frequency
Bar availability
Police Station availability
Police frequency
Model
As of now we are trying to predict SAFE/UNSAFE which is logistic regression.
